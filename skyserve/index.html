Galley FAQ

🍹 Adding Drinks: Tap Seat, Select Drink.

💤 Statuses: Mark someone sleeping or skipping. If they wake up and order, just add a drink and the status auto-clears.

⌨️ Custom Orders: Tap the Other ⌨️ button to type it in manually.

🍱 Meals: Check "Meals" to show food buttons. Tap Edit to set inventory. Clearing a seat returns the meal.

💾 Auto-Save: Your flight is constantly saved locally. It will not erase until you hit NEW FLIGHT.
