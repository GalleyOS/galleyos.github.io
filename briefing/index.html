App Features FAQ

🔐 Door Code: On the Dashboard, the code is hidden. Tap the text to reveal it.

⚠️ MELs & Catering: Check the boxes on page 2 to reveal text areas for broken items or missing food. These highlight on your dashboard.

✉️ CYA Notes: The "Email Notes" button grabs your flight info, crew names, and notes, and builds an email draft for your records.

🔄 Reset Buttons: Use "Keep Crew" to clear the board but save your FA/Pilot names for the next leg. Use "Clear Everything" for a new trip.

👥 Add FAs: Tap "+ ADD ANOTHER FA" on the first page to add more crew members for larger aircraft.
